Cathal Jones

General Manager

Cathal Jones has been with us since 2005. He is our current General Manager and is responsible for the day to day running of our business. He has previously worked as a manager in Mallow gyms. Cathal Jones holds several degrees. He has transformed with the way we run our gym's. He is regarded as one of the greatest managers of all time. Today with his experience and knowledge in both the fitness and business industry he is a vital asset to our gyms.
